Hi there, I'm Dr. Heng Yin!

I am an associate professor in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at UC Riverside. I am a recipient of 2011 NSF CAREER award. My research interests lie in computer security. In particular, I'm endeavoring to develop new program analysis and virtualization techniques and applying these techniques to improve computer and software security.

If you are interested in system security research, please stop by or drop me an email. Check my Calendar before you want to make an appointment with me.
